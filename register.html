Join Mulapal Agencies Today

Start your journey to financial freedom by completing simple online tasks. Our registration process is quick, easy, and completely free.

1

Check Your Eligibility

Before registering, ensure you meet our basic requirements. Mulapal Agencies is open to individuals worldwide. You'll need a stable internet connection and a device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) to complete tasks.

What You'll Need:

  • Valid email address
  • Activation fee (KES 500 for Kenyans)
  • Payment method details (PayPal, bank account, etc.)
  • At least 1-2 hours daily to complete tasks
2

Complete the Registration Form

Fill out our simple registration form with your personal details. This information helps us create your account and ensure you receive payments correctly. All information is kept secure and confidential.

Be prepared to provide:

  • Full legal name (as it appears on your ID)
  • Country of residence
  • Contact information
  • Preferred payment method details
3

Activate Your Account

After submitting your registration details, login to your account and pay registration fee using any of the methods provided. Click the link in the email to activate your account. For security purposes, we may require additional identity verification.

Once your payment is confirmed:

  • You'll recieve a confirmation email with further details
  • Login to your account
  • Perform Tasks and start earning

What Happens After Registration?

Once your account is verified, you'll gain immediate access to our member dashboard where you can:

  • Browse available tasks and their payout amounts
  • Track your earnings in real-time
  • Access training materials and tutorials
  • Connect with our support team
  • Set up your payment preferences

We recommend new members start with our "Beginner Tasks" to familiarize themselves with our platform. These simpler tasks help you build confidence and understand our system before moving on to higher-paying opportunities.

Within 24 hours of registration, you'll receive a welcome email with tips for maximizing your earnings and important resources to help you succeed.
